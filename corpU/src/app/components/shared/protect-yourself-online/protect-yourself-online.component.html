Protect Yourself Online

At CorpU, we take the protection of your personal information seriously. Here are some tips to help you protect yourself when using the internet:

Use strong passwords

Choose passwords that are at least 8 characters long and include a mix of upper and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

Be cautious with links

Avoid clicking on links from unknown sources, and always check the URL before entering personal information or login credentials.

Keep your software up to date

Make sure your computer's operating system, web browser, and antivirus software are all up to date with the latest security patches.

Stay informed

Be aware of the latest online threats and scams, and stay up to date with security news and best practices.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy or security when using CorpU, please don't hesitate to contact us at info@corpu.com.
